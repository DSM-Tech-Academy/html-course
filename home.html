He has shown you, O man, what is good;
And what does the Lord require of you
But to do justly,
To love mercy,
And to walk humbly with your God?

Micah 6:8

There is so many benifits to getting outside and spending time in nature, that everyone should make plans to be out there as much as possible. First, new studies have shown that getting outside lowers your blood pressure and relaxes your body. Being out in the woods helps you regain perspective on life. So many things to see and learn out there, we would all do well to spend time away from the busy life in town.
